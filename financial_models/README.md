# 📉 Financial Architecture & Strategic Ledger Models

This directory contains the multi-segment financial models mapping the economic reality of the 3 climatic solution variations.

## 📐 Structural Accounting Framework

The accounting models strictly follow the **Indirect Method** to bridge the gap between accrual profits and physical treasury liquidity.

### Key Analytical Assumptions:
* **Tax Rate:** 25% statutory corporate tax applied strictly to Earnings Before Tax (EBT).
* **Depreciation Method:** Straight-Line Method (SLM) over a 10-year useful life asset profile, assuming zero scrap value.
* **Discounting Metric (NPV):** Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) pegged at a 12% baseline hurdle rate.
